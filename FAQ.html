What is a ClientHub?

ClientHubs is a powerful new, low cost, online customer management platform that enables you to empower each of your customers with their own interactive, private, and secure online information sharing portal accessible 24/7 from anywhere in the world via their computer or smartphone.

Why do I need a ClientHub?

ClientHubs was designed from the ground up for businesses of all sizes ranging from local small shops to large enterprises to better serve customers.  

Maintaining an on-going relationship and rapore with your clients is vital to the health of your business and a ClientHub helps to grow and nurture that relationship. To learn more, please read the "10 ways a ClientHub will help your business" on our home page.

Do I need to make any special investment in software or hardware?

No investment in software or hardware is required and there is no contractual committment. You can canel your subscription at any time.

The secure ClientHub platform is a low cost monthly subscription service that is developed and branded exclusively for your business and accessible online by you and your customers 24/7 on any internet connected device including desktop, tablets, and smartphones.

Your ClientHub is branded with your own logo and company information while our system works behind the scenes on your behalf.

So, how does a ClientHub work?

Upon sign-up with one of our subscription plans, we will create a hub page for your ClientHub complete with your branding. We will also create a customized login page for you and your staff where you can manage your portal and we also create a separate customized login page for your customers.

A HUB page is the central component of the platform and serves as the main dashboard for your clients while they are logged in. The HUB contains whatever content you wish to share with your clients, including the option to dynamically display resources or links to resources that are permissioned to each of your clients.

Managing a ClientHub is very user friendly and you will have complete control of the hub where you can set-up private portals for each of your customers while maintaining complete control of your client's passwords and portal access.

As administrator you can private message and upload/download documents to specific individiual clients or all of your clients simutaneously from within the admin area.
