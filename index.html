What is a clienthub?

ClientHubs is a powerful new, low cost, cloud-based online customer management and self-service platform that makes it easy and affordable for businesses of all sizes to actively engage with their clients that enables them to share documents and communicate with each other across the internet.

With a ClientHub, you can empower each of your customers with their own interactive, private online, self-service portal that allows for 2-way sharing of documents and communications within a secure environment and accessible 24/7 from anywhere in the world via any internet enabled device.

The best part is...ClientHubs are so affordable that any small business can also have their own customer portal & you don't even need a website!

ZERO upfront investment & no contractual commitments are required!

There is no upfront investment requirement of any kind for additional hardware or software and no contractual long-term commitments. Your white label portal is branded with your company name/logo and accessible via any internet enabled device.  Each ClientHub features a unique login page for your customers along with a separate administration login page for you and your staff to manage all aspects of your portal.

White label branding 

We recognize that you have made a significant investment in building your brand and your online prescence. Each ClientHub is completely customized with your company branding while our service works in the background and is implemented without affecting the current design and functionality of your current website.

SSL encryption for every ClientHub portal

Your clienthub is hosted on our secure server complete with SSL encryption to protect your data. Simply refer your customers to the unique URL web address we provide or link to your portal via your current website. Our team is available to help make the process as easy as possible.                      

A built-in broadcast messaging system

The built-in messaging system allows you to broadcast messages with attachments to all customers simutaneously or send private messages to specific individual customers while storing a copy of all messages within their portals without the need of a separate email service.

A built-in quotation & invoicing system

The built-in invoicing and quotation system enables you to create, track, and instantly upload quotations & invoices directly into a customer's private portal while you are on the go. Customers can also conveniently pay their invoices while they are logged into their portals.

2-way uploading & downloading of information 

For added convenience, your customers can also upload any kind of documents, images, or videos into their respective portals to share with you and your staff while the system automatically and instantly sends a a private message to notify you of any new uploads and/or downloads.

A scalable platform to grow with your business

ClientHubs was created from the ground up for any businesses including local small businessess wishing to engage with their customers and can also be scaled up to accommodate the needs of large enterprises no matter how many customers you may have!

Upload/download & Share any kind of documents and information with your customers.  ClientHubs supports all document, image, and video formats

The ClientHubs system automatically sends email notifications to your customers advising that a new document or message has been uploaded into their portals and for added convenience, you can also choose to have the system automatically attach a copy of any uploads to the email notification.
