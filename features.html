Core arsenal of powerful features


ClientHubs portals are customized and branded with your company name,  logo, and contact information while our system works behind the scenes on your behalf utilizing an arsenal of powerful features that differentiates your business from your competitors, and helps grow your business while cutting costs.

We invite you to review the list of features available with each ClientHubs portal below.

Private & Secure Client Portals, created in seconds

Creates private and secure areasi n your portals that are uniquely permissioned to a particular client or clients.  Everything is structured seamlessly and intuitively so that both you and your client are comfortable within the framework. You can create as many portals as you need to accommodate all of your clients.

Estimates & Invoicing Feature > Online Payments

A full featured estimates & invoicing platform is included in each ClientHub portal with the ability for your customers to pay your invoices on line.  The system enables you to customize the invoice templates with your logo – Use pre-created items to build estimates/invoices quickly – Convert estimate to an invoice in one click – Instantly send to your client as a PDF attached to an email – Clients can make online payments with IPN (Instant Payment Confirmation) right inside their ClientHubs portal.

Secure File Upload & Download

Your ClientHub allows you to upload documents/files that are exclusively permissioned to a particular client or to all of your of clients, and optionally sends automatic email notifications to the client(s) about the new file.  Conversely, clients are also able to upload files they wish to share with you right into their respective ClientHub portals, and the system will automatically notify you via email of the new file upload.  Every type of document and image formats you may use are supported.

Secure daily data backup and clould storage

In addition to our SSL (Secured Socket Layer) secured ClientHubs area, all data in your ClientHub is automatically backed up daily and stored on our secure offsite cloud storage facility utilizing the latest security technology to keep your data safe and secure.  In the unlikely event of any data loss or disruption, your data can be instantly restored by the ClientHubs support team.

Import you existing client list to auto create ClientHubs portals for each client

Easily import your existing clients by formatting them according to the sample spreadsheet that we provid for you.  Upon completion of your  formatted client spreadsheet that we provide , simply click the import button, and ClientHubs portals are instantly created, logins are assigned, re-directs programmed, etc…. It all happens automatically!

Allow your customers to register themeselves

An optional custom registration form allows your clients to register themselves and once approved by you or your staff, those clients will be automatically set-up with a ClientHubs just as if they were added manually or imported. This allows your customer to choose their own username/password, and makes the process easier if you should choose to use this feature.

Simple Process to Add Clients > Customize the Data You Collect

Create custom fields to collect the data that’s relevant to YOUR business on the client set-up form, and display those fields inside the ClientHub portal.  The system allows you to easily assign a username/password for your clients and automatically send an email welcome/notification to the client with the login details using a pre-formatted email template that you control.

Create client circles

ClientHubs allows you to gather your clients into Circles, and then use those associations to easily assign permissions to files & portal pages for a wide range of clients with one simple step.  Each individual resource can be customized to be accessible by entire circles, or only select clients/members.  It’s all up to you!

Assign members of your staff as managers

The Client Manager feature allows the option to designate a “manager” for each client that is added.  All communications and file uploads associated with that client will be directed to the appropriate client manager.  Each client/member can have multiple managers, and each manager can have multiple clients.  You can also assign them with their own unique credentials and maintain all the permissions and access to the parent ClientHub, but using a separate login.  All logins are recorded and tracked in the "login log" and can viewed or printed at any time for administrative purposes.

Implement a portal for your business without affecting your current website

The implementation of a ClientHub will not disrupt the design or functionality of your current website.   In fact, you can activate a ClientHub portal for your business even if you don't have a website!   We have several options available to you that include: 

1.    Adding a simple link from your website to your SSL secure branded      ClientHub hosted on the ClientHubs.com servers.
2.  Host a ClientHub portal right on your own domain by transfering your website to be hosted on the ClientHubs server...and enjoy FREE hosting.
3.   Our third option is for businesses that do not have a website or require an update to their existing site with FREE websitehosting.

Intuitive file managment & categorization

Permits categorizing of files so that you and youcan easily categorize and organize the files that you’re sharing with your customers.

Ability to edit category names, re-assign files to categories, and sort category names using drag/drop interface

Categories can be optionally assigned to clients/members and Circles, allowing you to automatically provide access to large groups of files at one time.

Mobile friendly accessibility

Gone are the days whencustomers had to be sitting at their computers to access portals to view new updates, documents, and communications.

ClientHubs portal are developed using the latest in responsive web design to allow instant acessibility via any internet enabled device including tablets and smartphones.  

Your customers will enjoy the convenience that a ClietnHubs portal provides while they are on the go.

Private messaging 

Included with each ClientHub is a proprietary private messaging system that allows you to communicate with your clients in total privacy, right inside the client portal area.  Send a private message to one specific customer or broadcast a message to all of your customers simutaneously with one key stroke.

Emails are sent containing the content of the private message along with a one-click link that allows you to respond securely to that message.

Client login logs and alerts

The ClientHubs system lets you know when clients login to their portal successfully, so you’ll know that your system is working correctly, and who is accessing their customer portals.  

The system will also alert you to any login failures as well if you choose to activate this feature so that you can quickly follow-up and offer your customers instant assistance.  This level of customer service is sure to impress your customers which leads to increased customer loyalty and possible referrals to friends, family, and business associates.

Easily keep your clients updated in real time


Inform your customers instantly whenever you have updated their portals with a new an invoice, a new document,  or a private message with instant auto generated email notifications.   This time-saving feature allows you to optionally attach a copy of the new document or communication to the email notification and give your customer s the convenience to view the update immediately without having to login to their respective portals.

Customized admin and customer login pages

We create two different professionaly customized login pages with each ClientHub; one for your customers and one for your and your staff.    The login pages will be modified to reflect your company’s image with your logo, background color & text colours.
