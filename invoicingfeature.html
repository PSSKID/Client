Each ClientHub includes the quotation & invoicing feature

Generate quotations & invoices on the go inside your ClientHub & instantly send them to your customers via an
auto-generated notification email with the option for your customers to make payment inside their portals.

  • Create Invoices
  • Create Quotations
  • Branding
  • Online Payments
  • Withdraw
  • Add funds

Create custom invoices

Create one-time invoices or create recurring invoice profiles which auto-generate a new invoice according to your preferences

  • Send every week, every month, or however you decide.

  • Accumulating invoice feature lets you create invoicing profile that you can add to as you go along before you send it out and/or store in customer's portal.

  • Send PDF quotations & invoices attached to auto-generated customizable emails .

Create quotations on the go

Create a quotation on the go for any work or service your customer requests even while you are on location using your laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

  • Convert the quotation to an invoice when approved and email to client in one click

  • Pre-format often used billable items to quickly create estimates & invoices

  • LSend the estimate or invoice as an attachment via you customized auto-generated notification emails

Customized with your branding

The quotation and invoice templates are fully customizable with your logo and layout of your preference.

  • Set-up tax rates and discounts that meet your requirements.

  • Supports partial payments so that you clients an pay a bit at a time while contolling minimum amounts payable

  • LCreate one estimate/invoice and assign to multiple clients so that each client has a unique estimate or invoice.

Collect payments within portal

Allows customers to pay their invoices online using your choice of third party gateways.

  • Automatically stores invoices/estimates in customer's portal

  • Customers can download invoices and/or pay immediately within their portal

  • Supports IPN (Instant Payment Notification) to confirm and mark invoices as paid.  Also, optionally triggers a thank you email to send to customers.
