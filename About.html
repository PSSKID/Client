About Us

ClientHubs is a wholly owned subsidiary of MEGA Marketing International.   MEGA specializes in providing online marketing consultation, and website development & hosting management services for small to medium sized businesses since 2005.

Services include all aspects of web design and hosting, online portal development, and internet marketing including SEO, graphic design, and promotional video development.

Industries served include the security alarm industry, local retailers, medical specialties, food and restaurant services, publishing, automotive, financial and education services, real estate, and personal care services.

Our Manifesto
At ClientHubs, we focus on helping local small to medium size businesses with our powerful and affordable branded, secure, and interactive customer management portal service.  Customer satisfaction is our primary goal along with helping  our customers achieve their business objectives & long-term growth.

Our Vision
Our vision is to be the perfect bridge between our clients and their customers via our unique online portal solution that promotes a higher level of engagment with their customers & improved communications that help reduce attrition & grow their respective businesses.

Our Mission
Our mission is to equip our clients with a powerful, low cost, private, secure, and custom branded online information management & sharing self-help platform that connects our client with their respective customers in order to better serve their needs on a timely basis
