Website Hosting Policy
ClientHubs offers discounted webhosting for clients who wish to host their business website and ClientHubs portal together on the Client's domain URL as described in Option Plans 2 and 3 at a rate of $10 per month along with a $5 /month maintenance fee and a website size limitation.   Client hosted websites are required to be hosted on the ClientHubs’ servers in order to maintain the security and integrity of ClientHubs portals and to ensure that they operate efficiently and with minimal downtime.  No exceptions to this policy will be accepted.   

The $5/month maintenance fee will be applied to the selected monthly portal subscription and covers the cost of making minor updates to the website when/if required during the course of a paid subscription and may not exceed more than three (3) updates per calendar year.  

A minor update is defined as minor changes to existing text and/or replacement of existing images both of which must be provided by the client in writing at the time of the request. 

A major update is defined as any changes that requires major revisions to the website design, full page content, and/or functionality of the website.  In the event of a major change request, ClientHubs will provide a quotation subject to the Client’s written acceptance and payment prior to the commencement of any major website update.

The website size limitation is set at five (7) web pages although some exceptions may be made and is purely at the discretion of Clienthubs management.   

Website hosting will continue as a paid monthly ClientHubs subscription until which time the subscription is cancelled by the client in writing.  ClientHubs reserves the right to suspend hosting services for both the website and portal services in the event of non-payment of of the monthly subscription and will immediately be put back online upon payment of any outstanding monthly subscription fees.

Website Development TAB PLAN subscription  (Applicable to Option Plan 3 only)
For clients looking to order a new website via ClientHubs' Option Plan 3, a TAB PLAN subscription is available to all clients to help defer the cost of ordering a new website and all clients have two (2) options as follows:

1.  Clients can choose to make a one (1) time payment of $720 to cover the cost of a new five (5) page website with $10/month hosting, or

2.  Clients can sign-up for the TAB PLAN to help subsidize the cost of a new website over a peroid of 24 months consisting of 24 equal monthly payments of $45/month including the $10/month website hosting fee and the $5/month maintenance fees.  

The monthly subscription charge will be added to the monthly portal plan subscription for the initial 24 month period at which point, the TAB will have been paid off and the monthly portal subscription only will apply beginning in month 25.
