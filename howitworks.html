How it works

ClientHubs has made the process of implementing a professional user-friendly online customer portal for your business quick, easy, and very affordable even if you don't currently have a website.

Each ClientHub portal is developed and hosted on our secure SSL protected infrastructure and independent of your existing website so that there is no disruption to the design and functionality of your current website.  

Each ClientHub is equipped with a private login page for your customers and a separate admin login page for you and your staff where you have control of all aspects of your customer portal.   A series of short video tutorials provide everything you need to know to manage your ClientHub customer portal.

No Website?  No Problem!

You can put a ClientHub to work for your business even if you don’t currently have a website. 

When selecting Option Plan 1, we’ll build your private self-service portal and host it on our SSL secure servers and provide you with a unique website address intended exclusively for your clients only.  

For example, if your company name is Acme Services, your unique login address might be something like "https://acme.clienthubs.com".  Simply provide your clients with your unique web address and your customer will be directed to your company branded ClientHub login page.

Allready own a website?

If you currently have a website, you can simply link directly to your ClientHub self-service portal without disrupting the design and functionality of your website.   Your webmaster can easily add this link to your website in a matter of minutes.  However, our team is also available to provide assistance with this.

Either way, your client will be directed to your company branded self-service portal where they can access their private portals with the login information that is automatically generated and sent to them upon the client's registration.

Need a new website or web hosting to go with your new client portal?

Perhaps your launching a new business or your old website needs a serious face lift.  If so, our design team is available to help you launch a fabulous new business building website while saving you hundreds of dollars!

We also provide outstanding secure web hosting services and the convenience of dealing with one supplier for your website, email, and customer portal management.  Transfer your website hosting to our secure servers and you'll enjoy additional savings of up to $200 or more every year!

We have 3 different pricing plans available to accommodate each of the scenarios described above.
